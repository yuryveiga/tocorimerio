    Little Africa Experience – History, Culture and Resistance in the Heart of Rio

    Discover one of the most important places in Afro-Brazilian history in an authentic cultural experience in the historic center of Rio de Janeiro. The Little Africa tour is an immersion in the African roots that helped build Brazil's cultural, musical and social identity. During the tour, we walked through historical places marked by resistance, ancestry and the strength of black Rio culture. We visited emblematic points such as the port region, Cais do Valongo — recognized by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site —, Pedra do Sal, the birthplace of Rio samba, as well as streets, squares and buildings full of stories and memories. Throughout the experience, we shared curiosities, real stories and the impact of African heritage on music, gastronomy, religion and daily life at Rio de Janeiro. This is not just a historical tour. It's a deep connection to the cultural soul of the city. Ideal for travelers who want to discover Rio beyond the beaches and traditional postcards.

    Pedra Bonita Trail

    The Pedra Bonita trail is one of the most accessible and rewarding experiences of Rio de Janeiro. Located within the Tijuca National Park, it combines exuberant nature with a light walk, ideal for beginners and also for those looking for a quick walk with incredible views. The route is short and well signposted, passing through stretches of preserved Atlantic forest until reaching the summit. From up there, the view is simply spectacular, especially the Pedra da Gávea, the southern area of the Rio and the Atlantic Ocean. Perfect for all ages, the Pedra Bonita trail is the ideal choice for anyone who wants to enjoy a mountain experience at Rio with little effort and a lot of reward.

    Rock Climbing

    Rio de Janeiro is one of the best destinations in the world for rock climbing, with options for all levels — from beginners to advanced. More than a sport, climbing at Rio is experiencing nature in an intense way, with unique views between sea, mountains and forest.
