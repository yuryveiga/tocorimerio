    About the Tour

    Share:

    Immerse yourself in the exuberant nature of the Tijuca Forest, one of the largest urban forests in the world! The Cachoeiras circuit is a relaxing but also exciting tour, where you can explore several waterfalls, peaceful trails and incredible viewpoints, all amidst the tropical vegetation of the Atlantic Forest. Accompanied by a specialized guide, who has international first aid training in remote areas, you will be led along moderately accessible paths, learning about the local fauna and flora. Furthermore, if desired, the tour can include a refreshing dip in the waters of the waterfalls.

    Pedra Bonita Trail

    The Pedra Bonita trail is one of the most accessible and rewarding experiences of Rio de Janeiro. Located within the Tijuca National Park, it combines exuberant nature with a light walk, ideal for beginners and also for those looking for a quick walk with incredible views. Perfect for all ages, the Pedra Bonita trail is the ideal choice for anyone who wants to enjoy a mountain experience at Rio with little effort and a lot of reward.
